## ⚠️ Supersessions & reconciliations

1. **RESOLVES Constitution #2 (XP).** The apparent conflict between Art. V ("does not
   measure XP") and the bait strategy is **not a conflict** — both live in this one locked
   rule set. The reconciliation is authoritative: **XP is bait (honey), never the currency
   of rank; Good Done/Livity is the measure (vinegar).** Constitution Art. V "does not
   *measure* XP" = "XP is never the status metric" — true alongside the bait use. ✅ My
   earlier open question #2 is answered *by the source itself.*

2. **CONFIRMS Constitution #1 (Good Done → Livity).** This source also says "Good Done"
   throughout — so both founding docs predate the rename. The rename to **Livity** is a
   real, later, code-level decision. Canon records Livity as current term, Good Done as
   original. *(Still needs Drew's one-word confirm that Good Done is fully retired.)*

3. **"Conferred" → "Witnessed" (superseded wording).** Lines use "the rise **conferred** by
   an Elder" and "**conferred**, never pursued." Drew's **Witness-not-Confer** amendment
   supersedes this wording. BUT the same document already says rank is "**recognised** by an
   Elder reading a pattern… judgement, not arithmetic" — which *is* witness. So: spirit
   already correct; the word "conferred" gets reconciled to "witnessed/recognised" in canon.

4. **Seed of Evidence Architecture.** "Why No Thresholds" + "a pattern of witnessed Acts,
   not a score, is the evidence of readiness" is the Evidence doctrine in embryo. Our
   EVIDENCE_ARCHITECTURE amplifies it. Continuity.

5. **Guardrails overlap Constitution Art. X.** Two guardrail lists, same intent. Canon `01`
   merges them into one authoritative guardrail set.
