THE FIRST ACTS CHARTER
A working instrument — not part of the locked Ark Constitution. Disposable by design.
Purpose. The smallest possible test of the philosophy on a real field. Not a launch. A pilot. The framework is finished; this is how the first egg hatches. If one young person changes, the philosophy is validated. If not, the field just taught us what the Constitution missed — which is the point.
THE PILOT — “Loop One”
Who.  Five — young people you already teach. No recruiting, no app, no abstract “10 people.” The rarest asset in the whole plan is that you already stand in a real field.
How long.  Two weeks — of sessions you are already running. One short loop, then reality teaches the next chapter.
What it costs.  A notebook — and a man who shows up. No tech stack, no badges, no points, no streaming.
THE DAILY FIVE — five minutes
1  The Word — one DaddyManism, read aloud.
2  The Mirror — one reflection question. Let them answer in their own words — Patois welcome, never corrected.
3  The Act — one good done that day, witnessed by someone. Not self-reported — a peer or elder confirms it. Service, not attention.
4  The Line — one sentence in the notebook. Theirs to keep.
5  The Look-Back — sixty seconds: who did you help today, and who helped you?
THE ONE TEST
Did one young person become more capable of standing on his own — and of helping the next one? Not: did they attend? Not: did they finish? Capability, not dependency.
THE THREE LOCKS — non-negotiable
▪  Safeguard — these are minors. Never alone one-on-one; everything runs through D.A.D.D.Y. Inc.’s child-protection structure. This protects the boys the whole mission exists for.
▪  Aim — build capability, not reliance. A boy who needs you a little less each week is the win, even when it feels like loss.
▪  Separate — this sheet is not the Constitution. When reality contradicts it, reality wins — tear it up and rewrite. The studio tells stories; the nonprofit tends fields; never let one consume the other.
The real product is not the program. It is Continuity of Good — the one asset no label can re-license out from under you. You don’t run this. You show up to it. The man matters more than the method.
