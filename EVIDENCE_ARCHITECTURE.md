## 0. The Core Reframe (the spine of this document)

When "Elders confer" became "Elders witness," it created a question:

> **Witness what?**

Not worthiness. Not status. Not rank. Not approval.

The answer is: **observable, corroborated evidence that real growth occurred.**

Three doctrines hold the whole thing up:

1. **Evidence describes; it never scores worth.** Evidence is *descriptive*, never
   *evaluative*. "You logged service acts witnessed by four different people across three
   months" is evidence. "You scored 87/100 on growth" is judgment. We build the first and
   forbid the second.
2. **The machine establishes fact; the human establishes meaning.** The platform surfaces
   the *pattern* (what happened, plainly). The Elder witnesses the *person* (names it,
   celebrates it, says "I see it too"). Neither one *judges* whether the person is worthy.
3. **Evidence informs witness; it never triggers a gate.** Progression stays non-blocking.
   Evidence is what a human reads to recognize growth — never a turnstile that stops a
   person from moving.

This is not a merit system, a judgment system, or a rank system. It is a way of making
real growth *visible* so a human can honor it.

## 1. Problem Statement

"Witness, not confer" preserved the human and killed the gatekeeper — but it left a
vacuum. If an Elder no longer decides *whether* you progress, then what is the Elder
looking at? And what stops "evidence of growth" from quietly becoming the very score,
rank, and judgment the Constitution forbids?

We must define evidence that:
- makes growth genuinely visible (so witness has substance),
- cannot be turned into a ranking of human worth,
- resists gaming without becoming surveillance,
- and honors the Father Test (capability, not dependency) and the Field Test (heals the
  field, not just the self).

## 2. Council Analysis

### 🏛️ Architect — *What is observable?*
Evidence comes in three layers, and they must never be confused. **Self-reported** (the
person's own reflections, journals, completions) — cheap to produce, trusted only for the
person's *own inner track*. **Behavioral** (what the system passively observes: returning,
presence over time, acts logged) — hard to fake at volume. **Community-corroborated**
(service witnessed and attested by *others*) — the gold standard, and the only valid basis
for service. The architecture already exists in miniature: the `Livity` model has
`status`, `verifiedBy`, `witnessNote` — service is *already* corroborated by design. Extend
that principle; don't reinvent it. Surface evidence as a *timeline*, not a *score*.

### 🛡️ Guardian — *Evidence vs. surveillance*
The danger word is "evidence," because evidence wants to become *surveillance*. Hard
limits: collect the **minimum**; the content of a person's private reflection is **theirs**
— record *that* they reflected, never mine *what* they wrote as data against them; every
person can **see their own evidence** (no secret scoring); evidence is **never sold,
ranked, or shown comparatively**. The moment a person's inner life becomes scored data
held secretly about them, the Ark becomes a panopticon. The witness sees what the person
*chooses to make visible* plus what others *freely attest* — not a dossier.

### 🕵️ Skeptic — *What can be gamed?*
Anything with a number that confers status gets gamed: XP, streaks, volume, likes,
followers, leaderboards. Self-reported virtue gets inflated ("I helped lots of people").
Single-event evidence gets faked. What *resists* gaming: **multi-party corroboration**
(several independent witnesses with nothing to gain), **longitudinal pattern** (you cannot
fake a year of trajectory), and **evidence that costs the giver something real**. The
defense against gaming is not more surveillance — it is **corroboration over time**. The
same reason Livity is "witnessed, never self-reported" is the reason it can't be gamed.

### 📖 Teacher — *Does the evidence reflect real growth, or just compliance?*
The deepest risk: measuring *compliance* and calling it *growth*. A person can complete
every day and change nothing. So the truest evidence is **change over time** — the
language in someone's own words shifting from the lies toward the truths; the move from
consuming to giving; doing the thing they once swore they couldn't. And — critically —
**returning after a fall** is stronger evidence than an unbroken streak. The J-Shape is
"where people quit"; the person who breaks and comes back has shown more than the person
who never wavered. Reward resilience, not perfection.

### 🌍 Steward — *Constitutional alignment*
The **Father Test** turns evidence on its head: the best evidence is the person needing
the platform *less* and serving *more*. Evidence that rewards engagement-for-its-own-sake
fails the Father Test and must be cut — even if it boosts retention. The **Field Test**
ranks service (which heals the field) above any personal-engagement metric. **The Circle**
means evidence must point outward, toward becoming an Egg-layer, a Gardener, soil — not
toward a trophy. And **"fathering must never become ownership"**: evidence may never be
*comparative*. The instant you rank one person against another, you've built a hierarchy
of worth, and the witness becomes a judge again. No leaderboards. Ever. Especially for
Gardeners and Faada, who by Constitution have none.

**Counsel consensus:** evidence is **descriptive, longitudinal, corroborated, transparent,
and non-comparative.** The machine surfaces fact; the human witnesses meaning; gaming dies
to corroboration-over-time, not surveillance; and the truest growth is the Father Test made
visible — *needing us less, serving more.*

## 3. The Three Layers of Evidence

| Layer | Examples | Trusted for | NOT trusted for |
|-------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Self-reported** | Reflections, journal entries, challenge completions, the person's own words | The person's **own inner track** (engagement, honesty with self) | Service, virtue, rank, anything conferring status |
| **Behavioral** | Returning, presence over time, acts logged, rhythm of participation | **Engagement & consistency** (passively, factually) | Judging the *quality* of a person |
| **Community-corroborated** | Service acts witnessed/attested by others, testimony of those served | **Service & growth** — the gold standard | Anything a single party could fabricate alone |

**Clean split (doctrine):** *Service is never self-reported. The inner work is never
community-judged.* The person owns the evidence of their own becoming; the community owns
the evidence of service rendered. Neither colonizes the other.

---

## 4. The Five Kinds of Evidence

**1. Engagement (Q1)** — *that the person shows up.* Returning, presence, completing one's
own reflections. Behavioral + self-reported. Weak alone; foundational. The strongest
engagement signal is **returning after a gap** (resilience), not an unbroken streak.

**2. Consistency (Q2)** — *that showing up is durable.* A sustained pattern across weeks
and months. Rhythm over intensity. Durability over perfection. Behavioral, longitudinal.

**3. Service (Q3)** — *that the person gives.* Livity acts — witnessed and attested by
others, never self-reported. The single most trustworthy kind of evidence, because it is
corroborated and costs something. Community-reported. This is the heart.

**4. Growth (Q4)** — *that the person has changed.* The trajectory of their own words and
acts over time: the shift from the lies to the truths, from consuming to giving, from "I
can't" to having done it. Growth is an **arc**, never a snapshot. Read across months, not
in a moment. Resists gaming because a year of change cannot be faked.

**5. Readiness (Q5)** — *that the next station is already being lived.* Readiness is
**recognized, never tested.** It is the convergence of the other four such that a human can
look and say "you are already doing this." The butterfly is flying before anyone names it
one. Readiness is never a gate (non-blocking) — it is a thing witnessed, already true.

## 5. Gaming Analysis

**Gameable — handle with care or forbid (Q9):**
XP and points · streaks (as pressure) · volume/post-count · likes, followers, popularity ·
self-reported service · single-event claims · anything comparative or leaderboard-shaped.
*(This is precisely why XP caps at Caterpillar and Livity is never self-reported.)*

**Resists gaming (Q10):**
Multi-party corroboration (independent witnesses, nothing to gain) · longitudinal pattern
(a year of trajectory) · evidence that costs the giver · the *combination* of behavioral +
community + time. The rule: **corroboration over time beats surveillance.**

---

## 6. Forbidden Evidence (Q11) — never used, never built

- ❌ **Money / spending** — no money buys rank. Ever.
- ❌ **Popularity** — followers, likes, reach, virality.
- ❌ **Self-reported service or virtue** — service is witnessed, never claimed.
- ❌ **Comparative ranking / leaderboards** — no human ranked against another. None for
  Gardeners/Faada by Constitution; we extend it to all.
- ❌ **A worth score** — no single number representing a person's value or progress.
- ❌ **Surveillance-grade data** — content of private reflections, location, message
  contents mined as evidence. Record *that*, never *what*.
- ❌ **Speed / competition** — growth is not a race.
- ❌ **Demographics / identity** — who someone is is never evidence.

---

## 7. How an Elder Witnesses Without Becoming a Judge (Q12)

- The Elder reads the **evidence the platform already surfaced** + the person's **own
  testimony**, and **names what is already true.** Reflection, not verdict.
- The Elder **cannot withhold progress** (non-blocking) — there is no "you may not
  proceed" to give.
- The Elder **cannot rank** people, and is **never the sole authority** — multiple
  witnesses can see the same growth, so no single person is a gate.
- The witness adds **meaning** ("I see it too"); the evidence establishes **fact** (it
  happened). The Elder confirms reality; the Elder does not grant permission.
- An Elder seeking the power to *decide* who advances is, by that act, disqualified from
  witnessing — the desire to gate is the tell.

## 8. Guardrails (must survive into any implementation)

- **The Circle** — evidence points outward (toward laying eggs, tending, becoming soil),
  never toward a trophy.
- **The Father Test** — privilege evidence of *needing us less and serving more*; cut any
  metric that rewards dependency or engagement-for-its-own-sake, even at the cost of
  retention.
- **The Field Test** — service (heals the field) outranks every personal-engagement metric.
- **Witness, not Confer** — evidence informs a human's recognition; it never triggers a
  gate.
- **Non-blocking progression** — daily content never freezes pending evidence or witness.
- **Transparent, non-comparative, minimal** — every person sees their own evidence; no one
  is ranked against another; collect the least that tells the truth.

## 9. Answers to the Twelve Questions (summary)

1. **Engagement evidence?** Returning, presence, own-reflection completion — esp. returning
   after a gap. (Behavioral + self-reported.)
2. **Consistency evidence?** Durable rhythm across weeks/months; durability over perfection.
3. **Service evidence?** Witnessed, attested Livity acts — never self-reported. The heart.
4. **Growth evidence?** Trajectory of one's own words/acts over time; an arc, not a snapshot.
5. **Readiness evidence?** Convergence of the four, recognized not tested; the next station
   already being lived. Never a gate.
6. **Self-reported?** Reflections, journals, completions — trusted for the *inner track only*.
7. **Community-reported?** Service acts and the testimony of those served — the corroborated
   layer.
8. **Behavioral?** Returning, presence over time, acts logged — passively observable.
9. **Gameable?** XP, streaks, volume, popularity, self-reported service, single events,
   anything comparative.
10. **Resists gaming?** Multi-party corroboration + longitudinal pattern + costly acts.
    Corroboration over time, not surveillance.
11. **Never used?** Money, popularity, self-reported virtue, rankings/leaderboards, a worth
    score, surveillance data, speed, demographics.
12. **Witness without judging?** Name what's already true from surfaced evidence + testimony;
    cannot withhold, cannot rank, never sole authority; meaning not permission.
