## RULING 001 — Terminology: Livity vs. Good Done
**Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Status:** APPROVED

**Canonical term:** **LIVITY**
**Historical / predecessor term:** **GOOD DONE**

**Ruling:**
- "Good Done" is **not retired because it was wrong** — it is the *original discovery*.
- "Livity" is the *evolved operational language* of the Ark.
- Both describe **the same underlying principle** — not two currencies.
  - **Good Done = foundational language.**
  - **Livity = operational language.**

**Effect:**
- **Livity** is the canonical term everywhere going forward: platform, curriculum, Journey
  Engine, Elder system, badges, documentation, APIs, UI, all future Ark materials.
- **Good Done** is preserved in: the historical record, the founding documents, and source
  annotations where historically accurate.
- **Source documents are never rewritten** — "Good Done" stays verbatim in the sources.
- Canon phrasing for future readers: *"The principle was first described as Good Done and
  later standardized under the term Livity."*
