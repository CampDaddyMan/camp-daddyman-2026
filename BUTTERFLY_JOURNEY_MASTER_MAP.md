## 0. The whole shape

The lifecycle is a **circle, not a ladder** (Constitution Art. II). Eight stations: five
climbed-and-witnessed, three conferred-never-pursued.

| # | Station | Movement | Days written? | Currency | Human ↔ Software |
|---|---------|----------|---------------|----------|------------------|
| 1 | 🥚 Egg | Discover | ✅ 1–60 | XP (honey, onramp) | **Software-heavy**, low human |
| 2 | 🐛 Caterpillar | Learn | ✅ 61–110 | XP→Livity transition | Software-heavy, low human |
| 3 | 🔄 J-Shape | Surrender | *designed, not "days"* | **None — human contact only** | **Human-maximal**, software-minimal |
| 4 | 🫘 Chrysalis | Transform | ❌ not designed | Livity (witnessed), unseen | Human-high, software-low |
| 5 | 🦋 Butterfly | Serve | ❌ not designed | Livity, deep | Human↔Software balanced; person now serves others |
| 6 | 🌱 Ark Builder | Multiply | ❌ (likely **not** curriculum) | Livity; sponsors + walks the next through | Person *is* the guide |
| 7 | 🌿 Gardener | Protect | ❌ (likely **not** curriculum) | None; no badge, no leaderboard | Person *is* the tender |
| 8 | 🌍 Faada | Steward | ❌ (likely **not** curriculum) | None; becomes the soil | Person *is* the soil |

### The single most important architectural finding
**The back half of the journey is NOT "255 more daily lessons."** The "one day at a time"
model that carried the Egg and Caterpillar **breaks on purpose** at the J-Shape. From there:

- The **J-Shape** is a *variable-length held season*, not a fixed day-count — it ends when
  surrender is witnessed, however long that takes.
- The **Chrysalis** is *quiet and unseen* — likely anchors/markers inside an internal process,
  not a daily streak.
- The **Butterfly** is *service in the real world* — more lived than screened.
- The **conferred stations (6–8) are almost certainly NOT daily curriculum at all** — they are
  lived, witnessed stewardships, by their own definition "never pursued, no badge."

So "365 days" is the *container of a year*, not a promise of 365 authored screens. The
software's share **shrinks** as the person matures; the human's share **grows**; and the
final stations have no screen at all. **This reframes the remaining work entirely** — and is
the first thing that needs Drew's confirmation (see Open Questions).

### Two progressions that run underneath everything
- **Currency:** XP (honey) → nothing-but-human-contact (J-Shape) → witnessed Livity (vinegar)
  → the person *becomes* the witness/sponsor for others.
- **Human↔Software ratio inverts:** the app does the most at the Egg and the least at the
  Faada. Maturity = needing the software less and the humans more, until you *are* the human
  for someone else. (This is the Father Test, drawn as a curve.)

## Station cards (remaining stations)

### 🔄 3 · J-Shape — Surrender  *(already designed — referenced, not modified)*
- **Purpose:** the old self stops gripping; *"I cannot do this alone"*; the hook sets.
- **Must happen:** a real human witnesses the surrender; the person is met, not processed.
- **Must never happen:** automation of the human; abandonment; ownership; minors in the open pool.
- **Output:** a surrendered person inside a witnessed relationship.
- **Transition in:** Caterpillar Day 110 ("The Hand in the Dark"). **Out:** Chrysalis, *inside the same relationship* (Decision J-5).
- **Human:** maximal. **Software:** connector / container / guardian only.
- *Fully specified in `J_SHAPE_EXPERIENCE_BLUEPRINT.md` + `J_SHAPE_FOUNDING_DECISIONS.md`. Not touched here.*

### 🫘 4 · Chrysalis — Transform
- **Purpose:** the old identity dissolves into "soup"; the buried/true self (the imaginal
  cells) takes over; transformation that *nobody sees and nobody applauds* (Constitution Art. III).
- **Must happen:** the person is *accompanied* through a dissolution they do not control;
  received, not manufactured (Caterpillar Day 108 already pointed here).
- **Must never happen:** gamifying it; rushing it; rewarding it with badges/applause (applause
  would destroy the very thing — it is the unseen station); leaving the person alone in it.
- **Output:** an emerged identity; readiness to serve.
- **Transition in:** from the J-Shape, *carried by the same Elder relationship.* **Out:** Butterfly.
- **Human:** high — the Elder *witnesses the dissolution*, does not direct it. **Software:** low —
  may hold quiet space / spare anchors, but content here must be **un-performable and minimal.**
- **Open architecture Qs:** Is the Chrysalis daily content at all, or a small set of *markers*
  inside a variable internal season? How does the app hold space without filling silence the
  Chrysalis requires?

### 🦋 5 · Butterfly — Serve
- **Purpose:** contribution and pollination; the emerged self serves life beyond itself; lays
  eggs; Livity, deep. *Not achievement — mission* (Constitution Art. II).
- **Must happen:** real witnessed service that heals the field; the person begins to *witness
  others* (turns from witnessed → witness).
- **Must never happen:** pride / hero-making; service curdling into ownership; performing
  service for status; the "I arrived" trap.
- **Output:** acts of Livity that heal the field; the person becomes a sponsor/witness — the
  threshold of multiplying (Ark Builder).
- **Transition in:** from Chrysalis (conferred/witnessed by an Elder). **Out:** toward the
  conferred stations.
- **Human:** the person is now serving others; the Elder relationship shifts toward peer.
  **Software:** balanced — witnessed-Livity logging (evidence, never score), connecting
  butterflies to those they serve.
- **Open architecture Qs:** Is the Butterfly "days," or a real-world service phase the app
  *tracks and witnesses* rather than *teaches*? Where does the daily-content model formally end?

### 🌱 6 · Ark Builder — Multiply  *(likely not curriculum)*
- **Purpose:** sponsors AND walks the next one through the full circle — the butterfly lays
  eggs; the economy made literal.
- **Must happen:** the person takes responsibility for a new Egg and walks beside them.
- **Must never happen:** downline / profit-from-below / ownership of the mentee (Constitution Art. VI).
- **Output:** a new Egg, tended — the circle closing.
- **Human/Software:** the person *is* the guide; software supports sponsorship logistics + safeguarding.
- **Architecture note:** this is a **lived role, not a daily course.** Conferred (witnessed), never pursued.

### 🌿 7 · Gardener — Protect  *(not curriculum)*
- **Purpose:** tends and protects all five climbable stages, without recognition.
- **Must never happen:** badges, leaderboards, applause, or it being *pursued* — "you cannot
  perform being soil" (Constitution Art. VII).
- **Human/Software:** the person *is* the tender; software's only role is to **not** expose or
  rank it. **The architecture's job here is restraint** — to build nothing that turns the
  Gardener into a status.
- **Architecture note:** the design challenge is *the absence of design* — provide no marker.

### 🌍 8 · Faada — Steward  *(not curriculum)*
- **Purpose:** becomes the soil the next Egg hatches into; succeeds when no longer needed
  (Constitution Art. VIII–IX).
- **Must never happen:** treating it as the top of a ladder; any status attached.
- **Human/Software:** the person *is* the soil; software has essentially no role. The circle
  closes: Butterfly → Ark Builder → Gardener → Faada → the ground beneath the next Egg.

## Key findings for the road ahead
1. **Reframe "the remaining 255 days."** It is not 255 screens. It is: a variable J-Shape
   season + a quiet Chrysalis + a real-world Butterfly service phase + three conferred,
   un-authored stewardships. The daily-content engine likely *ends* somewhere in the Chrysalis
   or Butterfly.
2. **The software recedes by design.** Each station hands more to humans and less to the app —
   the Father Test made into a system curve. Building *more* app for the late stations would
   *fail* the philosophy.
3. **The conferred stations resist authorship.** 6–8 cannot be written as curriculum without
   betraying their nature. They are recognized in reality, not delivered on screens.
4. **Everything past the J-Shape depends on the Elder system** — which is exactly what reality
   is testing now. So the J-Shape prototype is the gate: **the Chrysalis cannot be truly
   designed until the Elder relationship it lives inside is real** (Decision J-5).
