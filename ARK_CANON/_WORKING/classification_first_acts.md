   historical record of the first pilot. Its home is `05_HISTORY_AND_DECISIONS`.

2. ⭐ **The "Daily Five" is the ancestor of the Journey's daily structure.** The current
   8-part daily DNA (body, DaddyManism, reflection, challenge, Livity, journal, closing)
   clearly descends from Word / Mirror / Act / Line / Look-Back. Worth a one-line note in
   `03_CURRICULUM_BIBLE` crediting the lineage — the curriculum was field-tested in embryo
   before it was 365 days.

3. **Everything else restates existing law** (Father Test, guardrails, witness, separation)
   in pilot language — confirming continuity, adding no new doctrine.
